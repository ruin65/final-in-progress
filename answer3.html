SEVERE ANXIETY

You with severe anxiety may experience constant
and overwhelming anxiety symptoms that
significantly impact your daily lives. You may
struggle with sleep disturbances, panic attacks,
and avoidance behaviors..

SUGGESTIONS
Seek professional help: Consult with a mental health professional who can
assess your condition and recommend appropriate treatments, such as
therapy, medication, or a combination of both.

Medication: In some cases, doctors may prescribe anti-anxiety medications
or antidepressants to help manage severe anxiety symptoms. It's important
to follow your healthcare provider's guidance and closely monitor any
potential side effects.

Therapy: Consider therapy options like cognitive-behavioral therapy (CBT),
exposure therapy, or dialectical behavior therapy (DBT) to address underlying
causes of anxiety and develop coping strategies.

Self-care and support: Prioritize self-care activities that promote relaxation
and emotional well-being, such as practicing mindfulness, engaging in
hobbies, or connecting with supportive friends and family.

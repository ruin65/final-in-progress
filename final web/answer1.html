MILD ANXIETY

You experiencing mild anxiety may have occasional
feelings of worry, restlessness, or unease. You can
typically manage your daily activities but may find
it challenging to fully relax.

SUGGESTIONS
Practice stress management techniques: Engage in relaxation exercises like
deep breathing, progressive muscle relaxation, or meditation to help calm
the mind and body.

Engage in physical activity: Regular exercise can help reduce anxiety
symptoms by releasing endorphins and promoting a sense of well-being.

Establish a routine: Creating a structured daily routine can provide a sense of
stability and control.

Seek support: Talk to a trusted friend, family member, or therapist about your
concerns and worries.

MODERATE ANXIETY

You with moderate anxiety may experience more
frequent or intense anxiety symptoms that begin
to interfere with your daily functioning. You may
have difficulty concentrating or completing tasks
due to anxiety.

SUGGESTIONS
Cognitive-behavioral therapy (CBT): Consider seeking therapy from a
licensed mental health professional who can help you identify and challenge
negative thought patterns and develop coping strategies.

Stress reduction techniques: Practice stress management techniques
regularly, such as deep breathing exercises, progressive muscle relaxation, or
mindfulness meditation.

Regular exercise: Engaging in physical activity can help reduce anxiety
symptoms by increasing the production of endorphins and improving overall
well-being.

Limit caffeine and alcohol: Both caffeine and alcohol can exacerbate anxiety
symptoms, so it's advisable to reduce or eliminate their consumption.
